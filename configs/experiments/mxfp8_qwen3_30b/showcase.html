linear

Attention & MLP nn.Linear → MXFP8 via _to_mxfp8_then_scaled_mm. Routers, gates, and the LM head stay in bf16.

grouped GEMM

MoE expert matmuls → MXFP8 via _to_mxfp8_then_scaled_grouped_mm. 128-expert non-EP GEMMs are split into ≤32-group chunks (torchao SM100 kernel cap).

all-to-all

EP dispatch/combine transfer MXFP8 payloads on the wire, dequantized to bf16 at the autograd boundary. Active in the EP · MXFP8 run only.
